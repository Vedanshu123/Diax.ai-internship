Dialog

It is the application for hospitals/clinics/practices, used by medical services and allied personnels like doctors, nurses, attendants, pharmacist and lab technicians.

Doctors



Timing

Doctors can set their consultation timings in any combination of a week and preferred time. This schedule can also be changed when the doctor is taking off or unavailable. This schedule will be used by patients during appointment booking.

Consultation

The primary goal of this application is ease of prescription. Diary intelligently recommends suitable medications, subject to the doctor's approval. Doctors have the freedom to fine-tune these recommendations, adding or removing medications as needed to tailor the prescription to each patient's unique requirements.

Appointments

Doctors can view their appointments in a calendar view for complete view of all their appointments. These appoinments would be based on the timings that the doctor has set based on his convenience. With this approach, doctors can effectively use their time.

Reports

Default reports based on doctor's appointments and diagnosis. Any report can be created based on doctor's need. All necessary support would be provided for such custom report requests.

Hospital/Clinic/Practice



Users

Apart from doctors, nurses and attendants are key personnel at a medical center. Mostly, these resources work in shifts. The application provides an efficient way to manage shift schedule for these resources. User can be added/removed, roles updated for restrictive or administrative access for efficent functioning of the center.

Out-patient

Registration of out-patients, if not using the Diary app is done. Queueing the patients for doctors visit and enabling patients visit in an ordered way based on the queue to avoid any frustrations with the patients.

In-patient

Admission of patients for serious conditions into a partiuclar ward and assigning a room for such patients. Provision of case sheet used for multiple entries by duty doctors for such admitted patients. Discharge summary along with discharge checklist is provided for complete care.

Billing

Diary's billing module offers a suite of comprehensive reports, empowering you with invaluable business intelligence to make informed decisions and drive growth.

Reports

One of the important system at a medical center is the billing and payment collection system. The application supports invoicing for all medical facilities. Also supported are quotations and purchase orders. Ease of payments using integrated payments and POS system. A complete billing solution for ease of running the center. Support for insurance and payment via insurance is also supported.

Lab

Entry and report of pathology and radiology tests with status updation at various like collection of sample, in progress and report ready. The report will be automatically tied to the patient using Diary app. Patient need to come to the medical center only if paper or physical copy of the report is needed.

Pharmacy



Billing

Sales and billing of prescriptive drugs and otc drugs. Help to find equivalent drugs and track the drug stock keep unit.

Master

Billing master is responsible to maintain the suppliers, manufactures, purchase orders, purchase order returns and payment for purchase orders. Tracking and return of expired or near expiry drugs. Drug quantity maintenance based on demand.

Reports

Key reports include Daily sales, detailed sales, due, returns, fast moving, slow moving, non-moving, medical/non-medical, expired and oustanding payments. Use the reports to get complete insight into pharmacy activities.

Admin

A key module/feature for role based access control, there by ensuring privacy of data and compliance to standards. Admin also facilitates the master maintenances of rooms, wards, lab tests and their charges. These charges vary and can be changed to reflect the current market condition.
